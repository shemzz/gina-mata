Program Components

Specially curated to drive the implementation of digital skills

The initiative is divided into 3 components focused on providing a gender lens approach that incorporates gender-sensitive designs, analyses, implementation and evaluation that addresses the barriers that persistently deny access across all aspects of participation in the digital economy.
Makoyan Fasaha

Enhancing the capacity of Instructors and teachers to deliver market-Relevant digital skills training around delivering modern teaching methods

Dalibar Fasaha

Integrating right based curriculum, creating a more diverse and inclusive workforce and for advancing innovation and progress in society.

Matan Fasaha

The digital economy, enables the use of digital infrastructure and competencies that can deliver innovation, and generate well-paying jobs for young women.
